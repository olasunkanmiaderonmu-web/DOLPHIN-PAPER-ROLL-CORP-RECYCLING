ABOUT US

Welcome to Dolphin Paper Roll Corp and Recycling

TRUSTED SCRAP RECYCLERS

Built on Trust. Committed to Excellence.

Established over 15 years ago, our company has grown into one of the leading suppliers of metal and plastic scrap, serving customers across the United States and international markets. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for delivering exceptional customer service, reliable solutions, and highly competitive pricing, making us a trusted partner within the recycling and scrap industry.

Backed by a team with extensive industry knowledge and years of hands-on experience, we combine expertise with state-of-the-art processing equipment to provide efficient, accurate, and industry-leading services.

We take great pride in the values that have shaped our business, integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence. These principles have earned the trust of our customers and partners, allowing us to build long-lasting relationships.

About Us

Expertise & Exceptional Customer Service

As industry experts, our experienced and dedicated team is committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and professional services. We prioritize safety in every aspect of our operations, creating a secure working environment for both our customers and our workforce while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

We understand that every customer has unique requirements, which is why we tailor our services to meet their specific needs. We specialize in the recycling and supply of a wide range of materials, including ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, and plastic scrap. To provide maximum convenience, we offer competitive pricing along with dependable collection and delivery services, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience from start to finish..

At Dolphin Paper Roll Corp & Recycling, we are committed to delivering high-quality products and outstanding services that consistently exceed our clients' expectations.
